OUR MANIFESTO

We are building the world’s most compelling & design-led game.

Simplicity • inclusivity • accessibility

Misfits is a new chapter in the tradition of stacking. We're bending the rules and we are pushing the boundaries of what's possible. With Misfits, there's no limit on the shapes you can imagine playing. No two stacks are the same. Face devilish challenges as you stack triangles on curved blocks, thread cylinders into hollowed out blocks, or delicately place that last pesky trapezoid. All you need is your hands. It’s nail-biting. Heart-stopping. Gut-splitting laughter.


But we wanted to go further. Misfits is about challenging the notions of what a board game should be — design-led, back-to-basics and welcoming. A game with purpose and intelligence. Do you hear that often?


It’s a vision of what believe and an expression of what we stand for. Like simplicity, inclusivity, and accessibility. You don’t need a manual for joy; we tossed out the rulebook and we won’t include one.


That's how play should be. Not stuck in the 80’s.
