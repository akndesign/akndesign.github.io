STACK AND STARE

The next classic
stacking game.

No rulebook, no rectangular blocks.
Only endless possibilities.

We are building the world’s most compelling, design-led game.

We're bending the boundaries of what's possible with a stacking game. Why straight lines and rectangular shapes? With Misfits, no two stacks are the same. Face devilish challenges as you stack triangles on curved blocks, thread cylinders into hollowed out blocks, or delicately place that last pesky trapezoid. All you need is your hands. It’s nail-biting. Heart-stopping. Gut-splitting laughter.

But we wanted to go further. Misfits is about challenging the notions of what a board game should be — design-led, back-to-basics and welcoming. A game with purpose. Do you hear that often?

It’s a vision of what believe and an expression of what we stand for. Like simplicity, inclusivity, and accessibility. You don’t need a manual for joy; we tossed out the rulebook and we won’t include one.

That's how play should be. Not stuck in the 80’s.
