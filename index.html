 builds on the legacy of stacking games. And yet, it’s a radical rethink of a stacking game can be.

We believe play can be enjoyed by all. It’s not just for the kids. Or the game connoisseurs. We threw the manual into the bin.

There’s no rulebook with MISFITS. That’s because it’s immediate. Sublimely simple. A game of suspense. Sabotage. All you need is your hands.

The game is a profound design object that’s simply magical to hold in your hands. And to share, on your coffee table. Or stacked on your bookshelf.

It’s an expression of what we stand for. Like simplicity, inclusivity, and accessibility.

A vision of what play should be.

We spent over three years in development to ensure that Misfits was as gorgeous and immersive from the first time you play, to the last round. From the beautifully arranged, forty American beech (Fagus grandifolia) wood blocks to the elegant slip-and-drawer design. Every part of Misfits combines beautiful engineering and a designer's love for meticulous craft.

Easy pickings.

Open up the tray from the slipcase to reveal the blocks delightfully organized in a puzzle-like pattern. This ensures every block is safely transported without the need for excess plastic or paper packaging. And, once you’ve finished the game, there’s a line design at the bottom of the inner tray to show you where the blocks return. No more lost game pieces. It is simply a remarkable and reassuring experience, from the first block to the last.
